---
title: "diag.home"
description: "Enable the motors and smoothly bring every joint to zero."
---

Enables the motors, reads the current joint positions, and moves each arm to the zero pose with a smoothstep trajectory (0.2 rev/s, 100 Hz control loop). The gripper is left at its current opening. Motors are disabled again when the move completes.

```bash
axol diag.home
axol diag.home --no-right   # left arm only
axol diag.home --no-left    # right arm only
```

Also launchable from the [diagnostics dashboard](https://docs.almond.bot/guides/diagnostics-dashboard).
